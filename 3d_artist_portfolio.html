About Me

I'm a 3D Specialist and VR Developer with comprehensive experience in creating immersive experiences for game development, industrial applications, and commercial projects. Currently based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, I've worked with companies like Siemens Energy and the National Institute of Industrial Technology (I.N.T.I.) on cutting-edge XR and virtual reality training solutions.

With a Bachelor's Degree in Video Game Development and Digital Entertainment from the National University of Rafaela, including an exchange program at Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences in Germany, I bring a strong foundation in both creative and technical aspects of 3D production. My work spans photorealistic modeling, character rigging, VR integration, product visualization, and architectural rendering for diverse industries.

As a professor of "Virtual Reality for Industrial Applications" and former Research Fellow in Augmented Reality and Virtual Production, I combine hands-on expertise with a passion for innovation and team leadership.
